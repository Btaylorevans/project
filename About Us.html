About Us

We are an online recipe book for aspiring bakers and cooks of all backgrounds and abilities. Recipes ranging from far away worlds, to lands of creatures, to even the universe of gaming - The Cookbook Cheatsheet has it all!

The Cookbook Developers

Our workers include Chloe, Hattie and Bethan. We manage the website and add new recipes to the online Cookbook.

Where did we start?

With a love of cooking and baking ourselves we would often talk about how we would wish we could cook some of the dishes and delicacies we read about in our fantasy worlds. Once we sourced ingredients and scoured fan pages suggesting how to cook some of these we decided to put them all in a cook book to share with other nerds of the world!
